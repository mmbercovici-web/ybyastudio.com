Kali

Kali is designed as a sequence of calm volumes embedded in the vegetation of Praia da Pipa. Spatial strategy is based on depth: verandas, setbacks and shaded joints that soften transitions between inside and outside. With a limited material palette and openings tuned to breeze and light, the project allows the landscape to remain present—through sound, temperature shifts and filtered views. The plan privileges cross‑ventilation and shaded rest, with small shifts in level and ceiling height marking use without relying on partitions or decorative effect.
