Villa Breathe

Villa Breathe sits lightly on the terrain, arranging living spaces as a continuous promenade between interior shelter and exterior air. Structure and joinery define depth—screens, eaves and recesses that temper sun and invite wind. The project relies on few gestures: local textures, controlled apertures and long shaded edges that let weather, vegetation and time become part of the architecture. By keeping the composition open and non‑hierarchical, the villa can host different tempos of occupation, from solitude to gathering, without changing its footprint.
