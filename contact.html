YBYÁ STUDIO

YBYÁ Studio is an architecture practice dedicated to site-responsive design, working across cultures, climates and scales. The studio develops projects in dialogue with territory, material intelligence and local knowledge, where form emerges from context rather than imposition.

Grounded in precise construction, environmental awareness and respect for place, YBYÁ approaches architecture as a process of listening, to landscape, climate, craft and community.

Founded by architect Melina Bercovici, trained at ENSA Paris–La Villette and Universidad Europea de Madrid, the studio builds upon international experience through collaborations and projects in Europe, Latin America, Africa and Southeast Asia.

For project inquiries, publications or invitations, please get in touch with a short description and timeline.
