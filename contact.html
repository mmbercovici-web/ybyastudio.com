YBYÁ STUDIO

Melina Bercovici is an architect and founder of YBYÁ Studio. She studied architecture at ENSA Paris–La Villette and Universidad Europea de Madrid, graduating in 2015.

French-Brazilian, her work has developed across different cultural and geographic contexts, with projects in Spain, Morocco, Turkey, the United States, Romania, Indonesia, Portugal, France, and Brazil.

She has collaborated with Nomade Architectes, Architecture for Humanity, Atelier du Port, Thibaud Babled Architectes Urbanistes, and Jacobsen Arquitetura.

These experiences inform an approach to architecture attentive to structure, material, and responsibility, where form is shaped by context rather than imposed.
