YBYÁ STUDIO

Melina Bercovici, founder of YBYÁ Studio, is an architect trained at ENSA Paris–La Villette, Universidad Europea de Madrid. She graduated in 2015 and built her practice through different contexts and scales.

French-Brazilian, her work has developed across cultures and geographies, with projects in Spain, Morocco, Turkey, the United States, Romania, Indonesia, Portugal, France, and Brazil.

She has worked with Nomade Architectes, Architecture for Humanity, Atelier du Port, Thibaud Babled Architectes Urbanistes, and Jacobsen Arquitetura.

These experiences shaped a way of thinking about architecture as structure, matter, and responsibility — before form.
