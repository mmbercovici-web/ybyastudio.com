About

Trained as an architect at ENSA Paris–La Villette, I graduated in 2015 and built my practice through different contexts and scales.

French and Brazilian, my work has developed across cultures and geographies, with projects in Spain, Morocco, Turkey, the United States, Romania, Indonesia, Portugal, France and Brazil.

I worked with Nomade Architectes, Architecture for Humanity, Atelier du Port, Thibaud Babled Architectes Urbanistes, and Jacobsen Arquitetura.

These experiences shaped a way of thinking architecture as structure, matter and responsibility — before form.

YBYÁ Studio was born from this path.
